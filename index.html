Understanding the World,
Geographically.

Welcome to the Global Strategic Post — an interactive intelligence platform designed to make global geopolitics legible at a glance.

History

Our journey began in 2026 at the University of Wisconsin. Amongst the growing misinfomation, we realized how tricky unearthing the truth can be. It became our mission to not only discover the truth, but display it in a understandable way. We offering our own analysis to help connect the dots together.

What We Do

We monitor news from credible local and international outlets across every region of the world and place each story geographically on a live map. Our goal is simple: to give you a clear, uncluttered picture of where events are happening and how they connect.

How It Works

Every pin on the map represents a monitored event — conflict, economic development, political shift, climate crisis, or humanitarian emergency. Pins are color-coded by category and updated regularly by our editorial team. Click any pin to read the headline, or click a country to see all monitored events within its borders.

Our Analysis

In addition to curated headlines from third-party outlets, our team publishes original analysis under the Our Analysis tab. These pieces are written by our editorial staff and represent our independent assessment of global events.

Editorial Standards

We source only from credible, established news organizations. We do not amplify misinformation or unverified claims. Our editorial team reviews every entry before it is published on the map.

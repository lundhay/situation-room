Global Strategic Post
Understanding the World,
Geographically.

Welcome to the Global Strategic Post — an interactive intelligence platform designed to make global geopolitics legible at a glance.

What We Do

We monitor news from credible local and international outlets across every region of the world and place each story geographically on a live map. Our goal is simple: to give you a clear, uncluttered picture of where events are happening and how they connect.

How It Works

Every pin on the map represents a monitored event. Pins are color-coded by category and updated regularly by our editorial team. Click any pin to read the headline, or click a country to see all monitored events within its borders.

Field Reports

Our Field Reports tab accepts photos, videos, and eyewitness accounts submitted by people on the ground. All submissions are reviewed before appearing on the map.

Our Analysis

In addition to curated headlines, our team publishes original analysis under the Our Analysis tab.

Editorial Standards

We source only from credible, established news organizations and do not amplify unverified claims.

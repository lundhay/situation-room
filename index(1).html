Understanding the World,
Geographically.

Welcome to the Global Strategic Post — an interactive intelligence platform designed to make global geopolitics legible at a glance.

What We Do

We track active global situations — ongoing conflicts, economic crises, political developments, and humanitarian emergencies — and pin them geographically on a live map. Each pin represents a living situation with a feed of the latest stories attached to it, so you always see the full picture of how an event is developing.

How It Works

Click any pin to see all stories linked to that situation, newest first. Click any country to see every monitored event connected to it — including situations abroad that involve that nation. Countries involved in active conflicts are highlighted when you click their partners.

Field Reports

Our Field Reports tab accepts photos, videos, and eyewitness accounts submitted by people on the ground. All submissions are reviewed before appearing on the map.

Our Analysis

In addition to curated headlines, our team publishes original analysis under the Our Analysis tab.

Editorial Standards

We source only from credible, established news organizations and do not amplify unverified claims.
